How I like to work with your team

We will adapt to your specific

needs and goals

We will brainstorm, conduct customer research, and define the product vision after understanding the user needs, pain points, and preferences based on qualitative and quantitative data.

We will break down the project and prioritize the most promising opportunity from the insights gathered. At this stage, meeting all relevant stakeholders to work as a team would be valuable.

I will work with developers, data scientists, designers, and manufacturers to build the initial prototype of the product and/or feature based on the proposed solution and gather feedback.

Based on the feedback, I will work with all the relevant stakeholders to develop the product and/or feature. Simultaneously, I will work with your extended team such as PR, marketing, and customer experience to prepare for launch.

Once the product is developed, it’s launched to the market. I will set a feedback loop to gather user feedback for future iterations beyond deployment.

After deployment, I will provide continuous support to optimize the product based on user feedback.
